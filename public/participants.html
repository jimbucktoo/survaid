Why Survaid?

Survaid is a versatile mobile and web application that lets you join research studies using your everyday smartphone. By completing surveys that utilize your smartphone's sensors, you can earn rewards while providing valuable research insights. Whether you're involved in a clinical trial or working remotely, Survaid is designed to accommodate your specific requirements.

Your input contributes to comprehensive research studies by leveraging the capabilities of mobile smartphones to provide researchers with visually rich data. When you participate in surveys, you become a vital player in improving the scale and precision of groundbreaking experiments, all while earning rewards for your contributions.

Powerful, Offline Surveys

Be a part of exciting surveys that utilize your phone's built-in sensors to create eye-catching data visuals. Contribute to groundbreaking research and make money by the hour.

Our offline data collection lets you securely gather information anytime, anywhere – no internet needed. Once you're back online, easily upload your data to our cloud server.

Browse through surveys based on pay rates, categories, locations, post times, and more. Read comments from fellow participants to choose the best fit for you. If a survey piques your interest, fill out the application form to secure your spot in the participant queue.


Real-Time Messaging
& Push Notifications

Experience a safe and real-time messaging platform with personalized push notifications, enhancing communication with your fellow researchers and participants.

Survaid prioritizes your security. Our app boasts built-in end-to-end encryption, ensuring your messages are fast, simple, and completely secure. Rest easy knowing we exceed HIPAA compliance requirements.

Stay connected and up to date with relevant information without compromising your privacy. Survaid's platform ensures you receive essential notifications and updates seamlessly, facilitating efficient and meaningful communication within the research community.

Build Your Brand

Craft a profile that's uniquely you, giving researchers a great impression and boosting your position in the Applicant Queue, the list where researchers choose their participants.

Let your personality shine by creating a customizable profile. Write a compelling 'About' section that showcases who you are. Profiles with longer descriptions tend to rank higher in the queue, so make it detailed!

Highlight your survey journey by displaying surveys you've taken part in. If you have expertise in a specific research category, you become a more attractive pick for researchers, earning you a higher spot in the queue.

Receive ratings and reviews from researchers, showcasing your past survey performances. Your feedback and rating plays a role in boosting your position in the Applicant Queue. Shine bright and let your survey successes speak for themselves!
