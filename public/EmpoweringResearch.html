Empowering Research

Survaid is more than an app. It's a game changing platform revolutionizing research. By granting real-time access to mobile sensor data and offering comprehensive data driven insights, Survaid transforms the landscape of data collection.

In critical sectors like healthcare and marketing, collecting participant data is fundamental. Surveys have long been a powerful tool for this, but the number of challenges have increased over the past two decades. Rising operating costs, low response rates, and non-response bias makes effective data collection challenging. Researchers now invest significant resources in creating questionnaires and offering incentives to combat non-response bias. Without this investment, response rates plummet, decreasing credibility. Achieving accurate survey results is harder than ever.

We empower researchers to gather real-time, reliable, cost effective data from participants' mobile devices, leveraging sensor technology like GPS location and pedometers. Survaid automates participant recruitment and offers detailed reports to researchers.

Participants are incentivized through placement fees and are rated based on their commitment and reliability. Flexibility is key. Participants can choose which surveys to respond to and when, even offline. Our Applicant Queue system matches participants with suitable surveys based on their profile, including expertise, reliability, and personality. Tailor your survey experience by filtering for specific criteria like pay rate or survey location.
